# 3.1.6 Pronunciation

- Level: AAA
- Principle: 3 Understandable
- Guideline: 3.1 Readable
- Understanding: https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/pronunciation.html
- How to meet: https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/quickref/#pronunciation

## Requirement

A mechanism is available for identifying specific pronunciation of words where meaning of the words, in context, is ambiguous without knowing the pronunciation.

## Definitions used

### mechanism

process or technique for achieving a result

Note 1

The mechanism may be explicitly provided in the content, or may be relied upon to be provided by either the platform or by user agents, including assistive technologies.

Note 2

The mechanism needs to meet all success criteria for the conformance level claimed.
