# 2.2.4 Interruptions

- Level: AAA
- Principle: 2 Operable
- Guideline: 2.2 Enough Time
- Understanding: https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/interruptions.html
- How to meet: https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/quickref/#interruptions

## Requirement

Interruptions can be postponed or suppressed by the user, except interruptions involving an emergency.

## Definitions used

### emergency

a sudden, unexpected situation or occurrence that requires immediate action to preserve health, safety, or property
